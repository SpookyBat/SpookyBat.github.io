About Spooky the Bat!

Spooky the Bat is a mysical Bat that only appears under certain circumstances on Halloween night.

  • It is a Leap Year
  • There is a Full Moon
  • It is below 50 degrees fahrenheit.
  • The population of bats is above the yearly average.
  • It is extremely foggy.

That is all that is known about Spooky the Bat.
